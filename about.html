About

Compass Technologies is a small product and engineering studio. We help teams run smoother by building integrations, dashboards, and workflow tools that remove busywork and make the right information visible.

What we do

We build software for operational efficiency: the unglamorous, high-impact stuff like connecting systems, reducing manual admin, and making sure the numbers match.

That can look like a dashboard that shows exceptions before they become problems, a workflow that replaces messy spreadsheets, or an integration that keeps CRM, dispatch, and finance aligned automatically.

How we work

We start with discovery: understanding the real workflow, what breaks, and what “good” looks like. Then we scope a staged build with clear outcomes and iterate quickly with frequent demos.

Clarity

Simple systems, readable numbers, and fewer unknowns.

Speed

Small iterations, fast feedback, steady shipping.

Practicality

Built around the team’s reality, not a perfect diagram.

Ownership

We care about the outcome, not just the delivery.

Where we started

Our first product is Huddle — a mobile app for coordinating group events. Building a product end-to-end sharpened how we approach real workflows: edge cases, human behavior, and the details that make something actually usable.

If you’re curious, you can see Huddle in our portfolio.
