About Us

Building technology that brings people together and creates meaningful community impact.

Our Story

Compass Technologies was founded on a simple idea: technology should solve real problems and make life easier for real people.

During a family Christmas gathering, we experienced firsthand the chaos of coordinating a group event—voting on activities, splitting expenses, planning meals, and keeping everyone informed. We realized there had to be a better way.

That frustration sparked the creation of Huddle, our first product. But it also revealed something bigger: the potential to build sustainable technology businesses that generate revenue to fund direct community action.

Our Approach

We build intuitive digital tools that simplify everyday challenges. Our products are designed to feel natural, work seamlessly, and bring people together rather than drive them apart.

Every product we create is built with care, tested rigorously, and refined based on real user feedback. We don't chase trends—we solve problems that matter.

Our Purpose

Revenue from our products funds direct community action through our non-profit initiatives. We believe technology companies have a responsibility to give back in tangible, meaningful ways.

Our goal is simple: build great products, generate sustainable revenue, and use that revenue to meet real needs—whether that's groceries for a struggling family, clothes for a job interview, or help maintaining a home after an injury.

Technology for profit. Profit for purpose.
