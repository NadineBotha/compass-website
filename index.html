Compass Technologies is a small product and engineering studio. We build integrations, dashboards, and workflow tools that remove busywork and help teams run with clarity.

Vision

To build practical software that helps teams do their best work: fewer manual processes, clearer decisions, and better outcomes.

Mission

We partner closely with operators to understand the real workflow, then design and build the simplest system that solves the problem: integrations, dashboards, and lightweight apps that fit your current tools and team.

Services

We focus on operational efficiency: remove manual steps, reduce errors, and make the important information visible.

Integrations

Connect the systems you already use (CRM, accounting, spreadsheets, dispatch, inventory) so data flows automatically.

Dashboards & reporting

Operational dashboards that show what’s happening right now: exceptions, bottlenecks, and the metrics that matter.

Business analysis

Map the current workflow, find the breakpoints, and define the simplest process that works in the real world.

Workflow automation

Reduce repetitive admin with approvals, notifications, and handoffs that are consistent and auditable.

How we work

A small, fast process built for busy teams.

1) Discovery

We spend time on-site (or in-call) to understand the workflow, pain points, and constraints.

2) Scope

We turn what we learn into a clear, staged scope with a budget and timeline you can trust.

3) Build

We ship in small iterations, with frequent demos, so there are no surprises at the end.

4) Improve

We keep refining after launch: reliability, UX, and automation opportunities as the business evolves.
